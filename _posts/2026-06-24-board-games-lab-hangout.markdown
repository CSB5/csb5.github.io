The lab recently gathered at the Level 3 pantry on 28 May for an afternoon of board games, organised by Minghao. 
Two games were on offer: 7 Wonders, spread across the pool table with cards and resources fanned out in full civilisation-building glory, and Evolution, where players shaped creatures and competed for survival in a more intimate setting around the round tables.
