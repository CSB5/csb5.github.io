On 10th June, the lab gathered at Peperoni for a farewell lunch to celebrate the four members who would be leaving GIS. Over several XL pizzas shared among everyone, we enjoyed good food and warm conversations while catching up with one another.

![](/images/activities/2026-06-10-farewell-lunch-and-card-crafting-session/farewell-lunch.jpeg)

After our delicious lunch, we gathered in the seminar room for a card-crafting session. The lab split into four groups to create farewell cards for the four members who were leaving the lab. Rather than making ordinary farewell cards, we had the opportunity to create aesthetic “exploding box” cards. Everyone took part by pasting Polaroid photos from lab events over the years, decorating the boxes with cut-out art stickers, and writing heartfelt farewell messages. It was a pleasant workshop and a great opportunity for everyone to work together and reminisce about the memories we had shared.
