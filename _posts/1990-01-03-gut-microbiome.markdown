Antibiotic use and our changing diet/lifestyles unfortunately have inflicted collateral damage on the teeming microbial ecosystems in our gut. We are studying how these changes affect our ability to resist colonization by [dangerous pathogens](/AMRITAS), and have long-term impact on our health, while identifying novel approaches to restore gut microbial ecology.
